Paper Heist is an exciting and suspenseful Spanish television series, tells the story of a group of talented and unusual criminals who decide on a grandiose and dangerous undertaking. The gang's goal is to do the most biggest heist in history, breaking into the Royal Mint Spain and printed 2.4 billion euros there. For a time, spent together, tensions develop between the robbers and hostages complex psychological relationships, and the general threat and tension only strengthen these connections.
